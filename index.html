Who We Are

Engineering Excellence
Since 2017

A Plus Consultants is a PEC-registered SMC Pvt. Ltd. company providing high-quality technical and consulting engineering services. Founded by Engr. Abdullah Anjum — a structural engineer with a Master's degree from UET Lahore and international experience in Qatar — the firm was established with a singular vision.

Mr. Anjum previously served as Senior Structural Engineer at Redco International Qatar, where he led landmark projects including the Mall of Qatar, Doha Mall, and Energy Center at Khalifa Stadium. He returned to Pakistan to build a firm capable of delivering Gulf-standard consultancy services locally.

"To provide responsive, safe, and trusted solutions with the highest-quality, cost-effective engineering, design and construction management services that saves both time and money."

— Abdullah Anjum, Managing Director
